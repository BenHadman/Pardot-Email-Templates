Syrris

2017 showed strong growth for Syrris with record sales for both Atlas HD and Asia. We've seen our customer base for the new Atlas HD growing and feedback has been fantastic.
 

A highlight of 2017 came from Spain. IFF, a leading global flavour and fragrance company, have worked with the Atlas Calorimeter for 6 years. A senior Process Engineer, Onofre Casanova, explained:

 

"We purchased an Atlas Calorimeter and use it every day to validate new approaches and test new reactions. It enables us to try small-scale reactions before moving to a large-scale pilot plant … We know that when we move from the Atlas system to the large-scale plant, we will get very similar results."

 

Alberto Lozano, and the team from MTB in Spain, have been working closely with IFF and we were delighted to receive the order for 9 Atlas systems in November 2017. This is a significant endorsement of Atlas by IFF and a tremendous job by all at MTB. Well done!

Dolomite Micro

It’s all about encapsulation! Analysis of 2017 showed approximately 50% of our systems orders were for drug encapsulation.
Dolomite’s approach to encapsulation overcomes shortcomings of conventional methods and provides almost 100% API encapsulation within precisely controlled, monodisperse PLGA particles, in a single reproducible step
